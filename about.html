ABOUT PROJECT

The official website of the Kurayami Shion Project.

What is Kurayami Shion?

Kurayami Shion is an original male UTAU character created as the center of a multimedia project. This project combines music, storytelling, artwork, voicebanks, and future digital creations into one connected universe.



Project Vision

To create a unique original universe where every character has their own story, voice, personality, and purpose. The project will continue to grow with new characters, songs, illustrations, animations, and games.



Creator

Created and developed by the creator of the Kurayami Shion Project. Thank you for supporting this journey.
